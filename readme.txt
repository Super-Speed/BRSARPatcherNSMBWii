This is a BRSAR patcher for New Super Mario Bros. Wii. Use this to patch BRSTM's to your BRSAR.

Load your BRSAR and all the other controls will be enabled.
From the dropdown menu, choose the song you plan to patch.
Press the music note button and press the floppy disc button. 

It'll prompt you if you want to make a backup of a BRSAR you've chosen.
